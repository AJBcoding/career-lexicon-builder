## Style Usage Patterns

### Section Headers
- Paragraph: `ps2557` (with 72pt indent)
- Character: `ss2561` (orange bold, 10pt)
- **Examples:** "EDUCATION", "PROFESSIONAL EXPERIENCE"

### Job Titles / Positions
- Character: `ss2592` (bold italic)
- **Examples:** "Interim Associate Dean for Student Success and Outreach"

### Institution Names
- Character: `ss2505` or `ss93858` (bold black)
- **Examples:** "California State University Long Beach", "University of Pennsylvania"

### Dates & Timeline
- Paragraph: `ps2532` (gray, 72pt hanging indent)
- Paragraph: `ps81930` (black, 72pt hanging indent)

### Body Text / Descriptions
- Paragraph: `ps81934` (plain Helvetica 9pt)
- Used for: Most bullet points and job descriptions

### Emphasis / Notes
- Paragraph: `ps2544` or `ps63797` (bold italic)
- Character: `ss2578` (bold italic)

## Summary

Your CV uses a **consistent color scheme** centered around **orange** (`#FF6D49`) for headers and emphasis, with **black and gray** for body text.

**Primary styles:**
1. **Section Headers:** Orange bold (10pt) with hanging indent
2. **Job Titles:** Bold italic (9pt)
3. **Institution Names:** Black bold (9pt)
4. **Body Text:** Regular Helvetica (9pt), sometimes gray
5. **Dates:** 72pt hanging indent pattern

The 97 total styles provide fine-grained control over spacing, indents, and emphasis throughout the document.
